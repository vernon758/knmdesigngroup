About Us

KNM DESIGN GROUP LTD is a full-service, forward-thinking design consultancy firm, specializing in residential and hospitality design along with a variety of commercial, institutional, recreational and Industrial projects. Our designs transcend mere aesthetics to embody the deeper connections between people, place, and purpose.

Since our founding in 2007, we have embraced the profound interconnectedness of architecture, interior design, landscape, and urban design, believing that these disciplines are inextricably linked in shaping meaningful human experiences!

our approach
  • We are dedicated to delivering an exceptional client experience from design to construction, with a personalized approach tailored to each client's vision and needs.

    Our innovative thinking and commitment to excellence foster creativity and integrity in every project. We embrace our social responsibility to craft environmentally resonant projects and collaborate closely with contractors and engineers for fluid design and quality delivery.
